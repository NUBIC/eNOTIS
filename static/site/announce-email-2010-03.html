eNOTIS is the subject registration system for all IRB-approved clinical research at Northwestern University.

Your studies in eIRB are already listed in eNOTIS. All investigators and coordinators may log in. Simply use your netid to see your studies.

eNOTIS tracks the subjects you accrue on your studies. Just upload your subject spreadsheet, or input the data on the study page.
